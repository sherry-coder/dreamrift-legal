Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 28, 2026

DreamRift ("we", "our", or "us") operates the DreamRift mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

DreamRift is designed with your privacy in mind. We collect minimal data:

  • Dream journal entries - Your dream logs are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to our servers.
  • App usage data - We may collect anonymous usage statistics (lesson progress, reality check counts, streak data) to improve the app experience.
  • Account information - If you create an account, we store your email address for authentication purposes.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the app's functionality
  • To track your learning progress through the 30-day program
  • To manage your subscription through Apple's in-app purchase system
  • To improve the app based on anonymous usage patterns

Data Storage and Security

Your dream journal entries and personal data are stored locally on your device. We use industry-standard security measures to protect any data transmitted to our servers. We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties.

Subscriptions and Payments

DreamRift offers premium subscriptions managed through Apple's App Store. All payment processing is handled by Apple. We do not collect or store your payment information.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat - For subscription management. RevenueCat may collect anonymous purchase data. See their privacy policy.
  • Apple App Store - For payment processing and app distribution.

Children's Privacy

DreamRift is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
